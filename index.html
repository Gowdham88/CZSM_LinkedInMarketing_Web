About Us

CZ Smart Mobility is a mobile app development and consultancy firm. The company has a vision to help promote sustainable transportation through IT. Wide range of services include Mobile App Design (User Interface Design/ User Experience), Mobile App Development (i.e., iPhone/iPad, Android), Mobile SEO, Mobile Games, Consulting and research. It has delivered innovative apps with neat UI and great performance to different sectors. It also offers services in Augmented Reality (AR) with real time backend. It has a small team with young and vibrant team members. The company has good reputation offering great service to clients in USA, Canada, Brazil, etc over the past few years.
